INaturalist API Observation Data Scraper

INaturalist is one of the world's largest ecological databases. With over 270 million specimen observations uploaded by users worldwide, it can be a valuable source of data on a significant portion of known taxa that would otherwise not be publicly available. With fully public access, anyone can retrieve their data for scientific and non-commercial use. Though they have a data export tool available, it is limited in the fields you can retrieve, as well as the total amount of data you can request. To solve this problem, I designed my own program to scrape their API, which, while not intended for data scraping, can still be scraped within their usage policy without issue.
